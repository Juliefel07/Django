How FindIt Works

FindIt helps you reconnect with your lost belongings in just four simple steps — from reporting a loss to safely claiming your item back.

Step 1: Report Lost Item

Describe your lost item and where you last saw it. Include key details and contact info so others can reach you easily.

Step 2: Report Found Item

If you find something, upload what you found, when, and where. We’ll alert users who might have lost a similar item.

Step 3: Receive Notifications

Once there’s a potential match between a lost and found report, both users get notified instantly.

Step 4: Verify & Claim

After verification, the owner can confirm and claim the found item safely through the system.
